About Me

For most of my life I’ve worked within the events industry. Backstage security, ushering, catering, cleaning, building stages. You name it I’ve probably done it. My last job before covid struck was building custom stands and marquees at major expos while working towards becoming a project manager that would travel across the country and New Zealand. Sadly covid took all that away.

It was obviously time for a change. I’ve always loved computers/gaming and desired to get into programming once again after having to unfortunately leave university 10 years ago due to family circumstances. That’s when I found Coder Academy. Over the years I’ve always loved building and creating things. So why not combine two passions into a stable and worthwhile career I can dedicate time to.

So far I’m loving everything I’m learning through Coder Academy and can’t wait to see where I’ll be within the next 6 months or even 2 years from now!!!
